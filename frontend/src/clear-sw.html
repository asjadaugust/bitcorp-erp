🔧 Clear Service Worker & Cache

This page will help you clear the service worker and all cached data that might be interfering with the login process.

📋 Manual Steps (if needed):

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I on Mac)
  2. Go to the Application tab
  3. Click on Service Workers in the left sidebar
  4. Click Unregister next to any service workers
  5. Go to Storage → Click Clear site data
  6. Close and reopen the browser tab
  7. Navigate to http://localhost:3420/
